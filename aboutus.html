About Us

Nestlé Bangladesh is a part of the global Nestlé family, one of the world’s leading food and beverage companies. Since its inception in Bangladesh, Nestlé has been dedicated to enhancing the quality of life for individuals and families by providing high-quality, nutritious food products. Our commitment to sustainability, innovation, and health and wellness drives us to consistently develop products that meet the evolving needs of our consumers. From baby foods to dairy products, beverages to nutrition products, we cater to a diverse range of customer needs across the country. Nestlé Bangladesh believes in creating shared value by supporting local communities, fostering sustainable business practices, and empowering employees. We are proud to be contributing to the nation's development through initiatives focused on health, nutrition, water stewardship, and rural development.
